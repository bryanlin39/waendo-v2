WELCOME

It's no secret that needing a root canal can cause you worry or concern. But it doesn't have to. Today's innovative root canal techniques are more efficient and patient-friendly than ever before.

We use ultra-gentle numbing techniques and state-of-the-art instruments that are virtually silent and offer minimal vibration.

If you are nervous or anxious, oral sedation and nitrous oxide are available.

We also partner with a skilled Anesthesiologist for any patients that may want IV sedation.

Together, Dr. Carina Lea and Dr. Ben Studebaker have more than 30 years of clinical experience and many thousands of successful root canal procedures to their credit. They offer the full range of microsurgical endodontic procedures, including root canal therapy, root canal retreatment, and apicoectomy.

Dr. Lea and Dr. Studebaker perform these procedures with the skill and soft touch you will truly appreciate, which is why they are trusted by many Seattle area dentists for their patients who need endodontic treatment.
